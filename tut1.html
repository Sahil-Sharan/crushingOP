About my crush❣

I don't know when it started, I started catching myself gazing at you more and more. At first, I just thought it's only because she is good in studies, then I deceived myself into believing that yeah, she is nothing special, she doesn't talk to anyone, she is just khadus and self-centered. I thought, "Stop looking at her, and you know falling in love is painful, it's not worth it." But still, nothing changed. I still found myself doing my best to catch a glimpse. I unknowingly started mentioning you more and more, to the point that once Aakash commented, "Why are you mentioning her? You got a crush on her or something?" But I just said no but realized that I am really developing a crush on you. But again, I deceived myself, thinking that it's nothing serious and it will be over soon. But nothing changed. Whenever you smiled at something Akanksha showed on the phone, all I could think was how beautiful she is. Then the dreams, I still don't know how a person with such a poor memory like myself still remembers them, but still, those dreams made me realize that I have developed a crush on you, no matter what I say. I know that the silent girl, who I branded as khadus, has made me fall for her, even if just a little bit. I knew that she is not khadus and self-centered as she smiles for her friend. I knew that she will always be there for her friend, and I knew that this is a fatal attraction for me. How I started wishing if only she can smile like that for me too. When we started talking, I was so happy that finally, I can talk to her. I asked for suggestions on how to talk to you. I never in the past ever cared what impression I am giving to other people when I talk to them. I just tell them whatever is on my mind. But this time it was different. I was nervous even after sending a simple message. I was nervous thinking if I crossed the line, what will she think and whatnot. I used to be so nervous during the lab, always thinking about how to talk to you and what to talk about, how not to show you how desperate I am. In which I was successful to the point that you even thought I was ignoring you. But even with all that nervousness, that day became the best day of my week. I used to wait for it all week, thinking finally I can see her up close, finally I will get to hear her voice.

My feelings after getting rejected ☹

I know you have told me that there is no chance for us, that I should not have any hope. But the truth is, the more I try to lose that hope, the more I find myself hopelessly in love with you. I know you think I'll end up broken, but I can't help but feel that having my heart broken for you would be a privilege. I don't have any expectations from you because I know love doesn't come with conditions or demands. I know that you think I am wasting my time, but all I can think is that the possibility of you is better than the reality of anyone else. All I want to say is that, "If by some miracle you ever find yourself in the position to fall in love again... Fall in love with me."
